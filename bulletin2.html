The Reality Dawns on the WASSCE Results

2nd December, 2025

By News Desk

Many good and decerning people of our land lament bitterly about our mental capacity to think and do what we desire as a nation.

Its quite disheartening to see cheating going on at the basic, second cycle and tertiary levels as if there is no tommorow.

The essence of education is to create systems that solve societal problems by having adequate knowledge of the area that one functions. The big question is if we cheat, how are we going to create the things that we desire as a nation? No wonder, our problems get bigger and bigger as a nation because thinkers are not there.

The current WASSCE result is the true state of affairs of our children in schools. They don't learn. They find their way onto social media with all sort of things. You can't blame them much. The society must be blamed because we live in a country where merit does not pay anymore. All one hears is "connection" here and there.

To most Ghanaians, education must be progressive and tied completely to industry. We must all go study how thigs are made. After Junior High School one must be seen for instance, in an entity where transistors are made and how transistors are used.

Where it becomes necessary to move on to Senior High School and beyond, the system itself must prescribe it based on the fact that one comes back to do something meaninful for the entity that recommends his continuous education. We can do better when our education sees a wholistic paradigm shift.
