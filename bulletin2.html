THE SUSPENSION OF THE CHIEF JUSTICE - MATTERS IN THEIR SIMPLIFIED FORM

5th September, 2025

By News Desk

The Chief Justice of Ghana, Gertrude Esaba Torkornoo has recently been under judicial attack for developments that has generated a string pulled by various intellectuals in both ends. It is very important that the average Ghanaian understands the matter for the good working of our constitution.

Technicalities in the presentation of issues surrounding the problem renders it meaniningless to majority of Ghanaians. As it stands, she had been releaved of her position few days ago in the midst of several legal banters. These were the three reasons the Chief Justice was removed from office.

1. There has been an unlawful expenditure to the effect of payment of travel expenses for her husband and daughter during a private holiday trips, together with other per diems and allowances in 2023. The commitee finds this as reckless dissipation of public funds.

2. The second petition submitted to the president borders on abuse of discretionary power, where her transfer of one Mr Baiden was meant to constitute misbehaviour in breach of Article 296(a) and (b) of the 1992 constitution.

3.Also presented to the President by the committee is that she bypass established processes for appointing justices to the Supreme Court contrary to the set standards by the Ghana Bar Association.

In all these, the general question asked by many is if such an eagle eye aluded to the other arms of goverment.
