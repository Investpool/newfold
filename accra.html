Accra is a cosmopolitan area and the capital city of Ghana. There are diverse economic activities in the region ranging from medium scale manufacturing industries owned by multi nationals, to small scale (one-man) manufacturing hubs. The Shai and Dangme areas too record a little agricultural production.

The area of interest to NEWSBOX is the small scale entities. These hubs are identified and mapped for a possible recommendations, support and expansion.
