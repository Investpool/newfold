DANGER IN THE FACE OF YIWABRA BRIDGE COLLAPSE

5th June, 2025

A Volunteer Carrying Younger Pupil Across the River

By News Desk

An old metal bridge linking Yiwabra community to ajoining villages has collapsed rendering school children a life treatening experience in attending school on daily basis.

In a chat monitored on Channel 1 TV with the DCE of the Aowin Municipality, Mr Stephen Morcher, he assured that frantic efforts are being carried out by the new administration to fix the problem. He however lamented over the negligence of the former administration in such a serious development that may become tragic.
