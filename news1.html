10 Arrested for Galamsey at Oda River Forest Reserve

9th December, 2023

ten illegal small scaleminers made up of three Chuinese and seven Ghanaians have been arrwsted over Galamsey activities in the amansie Central District of the Ashanti Region. The culprits were in a 3-day operation by the forestry services Division of the Forestry Commission and the rapid respond teams.


The statement said the Forestry Commission is steping upeforts to clampdown on illegal mining activities in forerst reserves across the country. Mr Huge Brown,the Executive Director of the Forestry Services Commision sharing details of the operation with Daily Graphic maintains that galamsey was operating in seven compartments in the forest reserve, a fit which is not good for the country.


According to him, several machinery were seized and other wooden structures were destroyed. He said seven out of the ten suspects - Michael Korsa, Basit Shizu, Amadu Musa, Prince Boakye, Amedodzi Kofi, Akwasi Adu and Shi Tiachao were arrested on Wednesday December 6 and arraigned before Bekwai Circuit Court on December 7.
