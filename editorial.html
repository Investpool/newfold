HIGH EXPECTATIONS OF THE "24-HOUR ECONOMY" POLICY

Politicians are resolute in their promise to the electorate. More often, further thinking is not applied to their concepts, making it open to failures and miscalculated realities.


24-hour economy as promised by the incoming administration, in other jurisdictions succeeded on account of many factors. Indeed our citizens touted it in many instances where they work tirelessly across few industries to make fortunes for themselves.

The metrics are simple: The 24-Hour Economy concept would only succeed at the back of expanded quality of life â€“ increased wages and salaries, lower cost of living, higher standard of living, lower energy cost, lower taxes and maximum security.


A case in point is a household which consumes 20 tins of milk a month. As milk manufacturing industry expands, there must be corresponding higher consumption of same household. When this is not projected, it would be very difficult for the concept to stand.


Export market alone for foreign exchange would meet the normal cyclical economic performance if the standards of living are not considered first.


Vincent Quarm

12th December, 2024
