EDITORIAL

ARTIST IMPRESSIONS, GHANA IN PERSPECTIVE

Many projects, especially physical ones initiated by constructors usually come with Artist Impressions. This tells the beauty of the expected finished work at the end of the contractual period.


Our communities and the state for that matter must have artist impressions at the camps of leadership and the entire society to inform our collective direction.


Over the years, one questions the National IQ averages of Ghana. Indeed it is scored at 58.16, according to a research, a level that is good enough to drive mind blowing fortunes for the country.


As this is not happening, and for the fact that there had been improvement in education in recent years, the ultimate is to make the artist impressions handy to every member of the republic to enable us carry a pride that we have a dream as a people.


Impressions however are public ingredients for development. When citizens get captivated by its contents, they put their hands to the wheel of progress towards its achievement.


It is very important to get the Artist Impressions of our country right and handy so that every able-bodied citizen can be responsible for its manifestation.




By Nelly Osekere

27th September, 2024
