EDITORIAL

POTENTIAL INJURIES AND DEATHS IN THE RUN UP TO 2024 ELECTIONS

Political intolerance has once again shown its ugly heads in the midst of various political activities across the country.


Its only God who knows who would get hurt, who gets killed in cold blood and those that would suffer all kinds of psychological nuances.


As much as party people struggle to outwit their opponents through uncultured means as insults, threats, open fights and gun shots, citizens must keep distance from them.


The big question asked by many is what causes all these?


A quick remedy to these situations is to reduce the financial benefits of political offices to the normal public office emolument levels such as that of teachers. There, one can experience a drastic reduction in hostilities that accompany political aspirations, and those who have the state at heart would be seen at work.





Justice Nkegbe

29th October, 2024
