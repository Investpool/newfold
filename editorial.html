ENHANCING SMALL SCALE ANIMAL REARING AND CROP PRODUTION IN OUR RURAL COMMUNITIES

Rural communities contribute immensely to animal and crop protein needs of the nation.


A walk around the countryside witnesses struggles rural folks endure in keeping the balance between livestock raising and crop growing. In a situation where a typical farmer attempts to make a garden of vegetables and the like, it becomes difficult for such farmer to raise animals at the same time. This is because the least chance the animals get they destroy the crops.


It comes to mind that there needs to be an intervention from government where logistics like netting systems, is developed and sold on moderate prices for these rural farmers to protect their crops from being destroyed by farm animals.


Its a common sight to note that mosquito nets supplied by Bill Gate Foundation plays an important role in this crop protection endeavours, quite contrary to the purpose for which they are supplied.


Frankly, the aggregates of small scale animal rearing and backyard gardening by families throughout the country can do enough for our food sustainability.


By Thompson NARH

2nd September, 2024
