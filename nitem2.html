2024/2025 SHS ACADEMIC YEAR FINALLY BEGINS ON OCTOBER 30

11th October, 2024

By News Desk

The GES has made a statement for the commencement of 2024/25 academic year in Accra yesterday.

According to the statement, there has been a revision of the school calender as follows:

Monday 28/10/2024 - SHS 2 students go home to end the academic year.

Monday 28/10/2024 - SHS 1 students end the academic year before this date and get promoted to SHS 2 on the said date to begin a new academic year.

Wednesday 30/10/2024 - JHS 3 students would be admitted to SHS 1 to begin their second cycle education.

In a related development, all levels of the single track schools shall re-open on Wednesday 30/10/2024.

Parents are encouraged to take note of the revision and act accordingly to usher in the new academic year.
