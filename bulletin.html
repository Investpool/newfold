THE GHANAIAN CEDI PERFORMS MARGINALLY AGAINST THE DOLLAR

By News Desk

10th May, 2025

The positive performance of the Ghana Cedi across the major trading currencies in recent times comes as the relief to both trading and the consuming public.

Unfortunately these is not the time of a price control system where the government would succeed in imploring the trading public to reduce prices of their merchandise.

One space for sure for the government to influence, according to views sampled from our outfit is the major shopping malls which must be made as the destination of shoppers if really reduced prices of things is sgood thing to experience now.

Indications from the Bank of Ghana suggest that more dollars are pumped into the system and also increase in gold reserves by the State and other fiscal measures by the current administration are making the trick.

Its the hope of all citizens that these development is sustained to increase purchasing power of ordinary Ghanaians and increase production of industries resulting in increased employment.
