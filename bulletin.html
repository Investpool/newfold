PROVIDING FARMERS MARKET TO CURB HIGH FOOD PRICES

1st December, 2025

By News Desk

An agric fair has just been organized by management of CITI FM where farmers and other related trades came to display their wares.

It is amazing to see the public praise such fit on account of cheaper prices for consumers to go with

One quick idea comes to mind. Why can't the government partner with the private sector such as the likes of CITI FM to provide bigger platform for such ventures at various locations?

Food budget for families takes the bigger portion of total expenses. This is because of the involvement of middlemen in the food distribution chain.

If this is done, farmers would get real value for their labour, citizens would get cheaper food for their families.
