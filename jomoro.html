LATEST NEWS

25th June, 2025

The Half Assini Police have busted Issa Morro 58 and Suleman Abdul Rashid 27, for mal-handling an infant over 200 Ghana cedis theft at Ankase in the Jomoro District of the Western Region

The 12 year old boy had been accused of stealing Ghc 200 and subjected to severe beatings while being hanged on rafters of a roof and in addition got his body smeared with mashed pepper.

This was casted on the social media by a benevolent onlooker. The police however advised the public to report such developments to the nearest police station.
