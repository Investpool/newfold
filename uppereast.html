Upper East Region with Bawku as a buzzling marketing center links two international frontiers.

Different kinds of products, both industrial and agricultural, find their way into the region. This is associated with the fact that it connects the sahel to the south, a position that drives an increased consumption of primary and industrial products.

It is apparent that much smoother commercial links to the region, by way of ownership and management of supply chains are required to ensure a sustainable business environment.
